Frequently asked questions

How do I play the game?

Via the game Tab you can reach the game. Choose the puzzle you want to solve. The goal is to order the pieces of the puzzle to a visually pleasing image by clicking the first piece and swapping it with the piece you click next until you finish the puzzle.

How do I change my userdata?

A change of the data provided during the sign up is not possible within the Webapplication. To change the data please contact if20b008@technikum-wien.at.

I cannot log myself in, what should I do?

If you cannot log yourself in you are either using the wrong userdata or you are not signed up correctly. In order to Sign up please visit the Sign up tab on top.

I forgot my password, what do I do?

Unfortunately there is no funcionality to get your password via the Webapplication. Please contact us via if20b008@technikum-wien.at for further instructions.

I can not see my highscore on the page, what happened?

If you cannot see your Highscore with the correct username or not at all please check if you were logged in correctly and reload the page. If you cannot find a solution after reloading the page contact if20b008@technikum-wien.at.
